Who we are

We are a locally owned and operated produce business in the Edmond area. We have a heart to serve the local restaurants of Oklahoma by supplying them with the highest quality produce and the best service possible

We are now serving our local communities by delivering produce directly to the people of Edmond and surrounding areas. While many have moved their families and businesses into their homes to guard against the Covid-19 pandemic, it is our hope to help Oklahomans continue to stay safe while bringing quality produce straight to them
